# ORACIA Risk Register

## High-risk areas

1. Misleading prediction claims
   - Avoid: future guarantees, 100% accuracy, fate claims.
   - Use: symbolic self-reflection, entertainment, journaling prompts.

2. Payment processor review
   - Merchant description: personalized symbolic self-reflection reports delivered digitally.
   - Avoid: psychic, fortune telling, guaranteed future reading.

3. Privacy
   - Do not send PII to analytics.
   - Do not log email, name, birth date, birth place, or report HTML.
   - Keep reports in private storage only.

4. Fulfillment failure
   - Webhook idempotency required.
   - Email idempotency required.
   - Admin retry route required.
   - Alerts recommended for paid-but-not-fulfilled after 15 minutes.

5. Refund and chargeback
   - State report is a personalized digital product.
   - Provide delivery guarantee: resend or refund if report is not delivered.

## Required wording

ORACIA is for entertainment and self-reflection only. It is not medical, psychological, legal, financial, relationship, or professional advice. ORACIA does not guarantee future outcomes and should not be used to make major life decisions.
